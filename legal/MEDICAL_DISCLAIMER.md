# ClearFast Medical Disclaimer

Effective date: March 4, 2026

ClearFast is an educational wellness application, not a medical device or clinical monitoring tool.

## Consult A Doctor Before Fasting If You
- Have diabetes, blood pressure issues, kidney disease, heart disease, or a history of eating disorders.
- Take prescription medication affected by food timing.
- Are pregnant, breastfeeding, or under 18.

## Stop Fasting And Seek Care If You Feel Unwell
Stop immediately and seek urgent care if you experience dizziness, fainting, confusion, severe weakness, chest pain, persistent vomiting, or other concerning symptoms.

## Extended Protocols (36h-72h)
Long fasts should only be performed with medical supervision and a structured refeeding plan.
