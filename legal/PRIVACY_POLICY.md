# ClearFast Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

## Information We Store
ClearFast stores fasting goals, start/end times, progress, history records, theme preferences, and selected app settings required to run the product.

## Where Data Is Stored
Primary app data is stored on your device. If cloud sync is enabled in the app, selected records may also sync through your iCloud account controlled by Apple.

## Data We Do Not Sell
ClearFast does not sell your personal data. We do not run ad-targeting profiles from fasting history.

## CSV Export
CSV export files are generated only when you request export in the app. You control where those files are shared.

## Data Deletion
You can remove local app data from Settings > Danger Zone > Clear All Data.

## Children
ClearFast is not intended for users under 18.

## Contact
Support and policy requests: https://github.com/harimalar/Clean-Fast/issues
